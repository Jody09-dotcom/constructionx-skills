# NHBC Standards Chapter Index

Pointer map for the main parts of NHBC Standards. No copied NHBC text. For full chapter content, subscribers access nhbc.co.uk TechZone. Chapter numbering follows the main NHBC Standards structure; check the current-year edition for any renumbering.

## Part 1, General Requirements

Covers general performance, statutory requirements, workmanship, drawings, materials. The framework under which all other parts apply.

- Scope: performance standards, statutory context, general workmanship expectations.
- URL: https://www.nhbc.co.uk/builders/products-and-services/techzone/nhbc-standards

## Part 2, Materials

Covers materials selection, storage, and use. Compatible materials, durability, traceability.

- Scope: materials fit for purpose, stored correctly, installed correctly.

## Part 3, Siteworks

Covers excavation, ground treatment, ground bearing capacity, ground investigation, protection of adjacent structures.

- Scope: ground conditions, excavations, temporary works, protection of trees and existing structures.

## Part 4, Foundations

Includes Chapter 4.1 Land Quality and Chapter 4.2 Building near trees. Foundations to suit ground and loading.

- Scope: strip, raft, pile, trench fill foundations. Ground movement. Shrinkable clay and tree influence.
- Common elements: foundation depth against trees, raft design on variable ground, piled foundations to made ground.

## Part 5, Substructure, DPCs, and Ground Floors

Includes Chapter 5.1 Substructure and ground bearing floors, Chapter 5.2 Suspended ground floors, Chapter 5.3 Drainage below ground.

- Scope: substructure walls, DPM, DPC, radon, ground floor construction, below-ground drainage.
- Common elements: DPM lap and seal at perimeter, DPC continuity, radon membrane where required, bedding and cover to drainage.

## Part 6, Superstructure (Excluding Roofs)

Includes Chapter 6.1 External masonry walls, 6.2 External timber framed walls, 6.3 Internal walls, 6.4 Timber and concrete upper floors, 6.5 Steelwork, 6.6 Staircases, 6.7 Doors, windows and glazing, 6.8 Fireplaces and flues, 6.9 Curtain walling and cladding, 6.10 Light steel framed walls and floors.

- Scope: load-bearing walls, cavity construction, wall ties, openings and lintels, insulation, timber frame, internal walls, upper floors, stairs, windows, doors, fireplaces, curtain walls, SFS.
- Common elements: wall tie spacing and embedment, cavity tray at openings, lintel bearings, window cill and DPC, restraint straps, party wall fire stops.

## Part 7, Roofs

Includes Chapter 7.1 Flat roofs and balconies, 7.2 Pitched roofs.

- Scope: flat roof falls and detailing, warm vs cold deck, pitched roof trusses, eaves and verges, roof windows, tiling, slating, lead and zinc, parapet detailing.
- Common elements: flat roof falls to outlets, eaves ventilation, underlay lap and fixing, truss restraint and bracing, fixing specification to exposure zone.

## Part 8, Services and Internal Finishings

Includes Chapter 8.1 Internal services (electrical, plumbing, heating, ventilation), 8.2 Wall and ceiling finishes, 8.3 Floor finishes.

- Scope: electrical first and second fix, hot and cold water, heating, ventilation, plastering, boarding, tiling, floor finishes.
- Common elements: ventilation to wet rooms, water temperature at outlets, consumer unit and earthing, smoke detection, plaster drying times.

## Part 9, Drainage and External Works

Includes Chapter 9.1 A consistent approach to finishes, 9.2 Wall tiling, 9.3 Floor tiling, 9.4 Wood block flooring, 9.5 Decorative finishes, plus external works content. Chapter numbering has shifted historically, check current edition.

- Scope: external paved areas, gravel, tarmac, retaining walls, boundary structures, drives, above ground drainage, rainwater goods.
- Common elements: drive falls and cross-falls, retaining wall height and drainage behind, rainwater pipe discharge position.

## Part 10, Appendices and Other

Appendices, technical notes, guidance documents issued by NHBC in support of the main parts.

- Scope: technical guidance, supplementary material, extracts cross-referenced from the main parts.

## Using This Index

This index is a navigation aid. To check compliance, the user must read the full chapter in the live edition at https://www.nhbc.co.uk. If the user is not a subscriber, the chapter is accessed through their employer's or designer's subscription.

## Revisions

NHBC Standards are revised annually. Expect chapter numbering, scope, and specific requirements to change year on year. Always confirm the current edition before relying on any summary.
