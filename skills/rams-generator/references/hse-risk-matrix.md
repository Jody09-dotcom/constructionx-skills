# HSE 5x5 Risk Matrix

Standard UK construction risk matrix. Likelihood 1 to 5, Severity 1 to 5, Score = Likelihood x Severity.

## Likelihood

| Score | Label | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Rare | Could occur in exceptional circumstances only |
| 2 | Unlikely | Could occur at some time but not expected |
| 3 | Possible | Might occur under normal circumstances |
| 4 | Likely | Will probably occur in most circumstances |
| 5 | Almost Certain | Expected to occur in most circumstances |

## Severity

| Score | Label | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Insignificant | No injury, near miss, minor nuisance |
| 2 | Minor | First aid only, no lost time |
| 3 | Moderate | Injury requiring medical treatment, lost time up to 7 days |
| 4 | Major | Serious injury, over 7 days lost time, specified injury under RIDDOR |
| 5 | Catastrophic | Fatality, multiple serious injuries, permanent disability |

## Matrix (Likelihood x Severity)

|  | S=1 | S=2 | S=3 | S=4 | S=5 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **L=1** | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 |
| **L=2** | 2 | 4 | 6 | 8 | 10 |
| **L=3** | 3 | 6 | 9 | 12 | 15 |
| **L=4** | 4 | 8 | 12 | 16 | 20 |
| **L=5** | 5 | 10 | 15 | 20 | 25 |

## Risk Banding

| Score | Band | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| 1 to 6 | Low | Routine controls. Monitor, review periodically. |
| 8 to 12 | Medium | Specific controls required. Work may proceed with control measures in place. Reviewed before work. |
| 15 to 25 | High | Stop. Do not proceed until controls reduce residual risk to Medium or below. Escalate to competent person. |

## Use

Score the inherent risk before controls, then score the residual risk after controls are applied. Residual risk should be Low or Medium, with a strong preference for Low. If residual risk is High, the control plan is not adequate and the method must be revised.

## Source

HSE INDG163, "Risk assessment: a brief guide to controlling risks in the workplace".
